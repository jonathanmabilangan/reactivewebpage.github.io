About us

Altus Capital Partners

A specialist investor and manager, focused on special situation and structured finance investments in South East Asia.

The firm, set up in 2005, started advising on and managing distressed credit portfolios in the Philippines and Thailand on behalf of institutional investors looking for exposure in these markets.

As the firm grew, single credit special situations and distressed real estate were added as core partner-investors for alignment of interest.

The firm is local. We are based in the markets we invest in giving us an advantage in sourcing, due diligence, structuring and capital recovery. Our investments are tailor fit to local nuances of our markets, as well as the complexities of the transactions themselves. Our asset management and capital recovery team allow us to price assets better, and diligence them based on historical exits and realizations, as well as present market conditions.

It is the main reason, that we are the go to partner of foreign institutional investors in the region.

Current assets under management include distressed loans and real estate portfolios, single credit investments, pre IPO facilities, mezzanine debt, turnaround and rescue financing as well as hospitality assets, primed for turnaround and repositioning.

The firm sees technology as the main driver of change to facilitate and democratize social and economic development. With this in mind, the firm set up its owner-operator digital private equity practice in 2016 called Altus Digital Capital.

The firm and its investments are managed by Altus Capital Partners Pte Ltd, owned by the firm's partners and the International Finance Corporation, the private sector investment arm of the World Bank.
